About US

What we are doing

Our Mandate

AGRI-ENTERPRISE

AEFA supports sustainable agricultural development through value added farming of crops. We're currently supporting three farming systems in the lower Eastern Region of Kenya, ie Cassava, Green grams and sorghum.We also support horticulture ie fruits and vegetables farming system.

WOMEN EMPOWERNMENT

AEFA works with marginalized women in giving them a voice to be heard appreciated, in partnership with county government of Machakos National government affirmative action fund, Aefa initiated the women food security and promotion of the use of energy saving cook stoves project that was highly successful. At Aefa we strongly believe and subscribe to the principle of women empowerment.

ENVIRONMENT, RENEWABLE ENERGY, CLIMATE CHANGE & PRODUCTIVE LANDSCAPES

The environment, very simply encompass the atmosphere, ecosystem, water, biodiversity and plant and animal life. AEFA creates environmental awareness and carries out biodiversity projects in the lower Eastern Region through the provision of leadership and encouraging partnerships in caring for the environment by inspiring, informing and enabling communities to improve their quality of life without compromising that of future generations. We advocate for farming practices that increase farmers resilience to climate change. We promote the use the use of environmentally friendly energy saving cook stoves.

Our Organization

Our Mandate.

AEFA listens to and learns from small holder farmer to identify their own needs through well conceived and sustainable approaches.

Our Mission is To empower vulnerable groups with an aim of achieving livelihood related MDGs at community level.
