Agribusiness & Environmental Focus For
Africa.

Aefa is a social Enterprise organization resgistered in Kenya in 2013.Aefa exists to facilitate the creation of a environment that is conducive for sustainable human development. We work with communities and in partnership with other organizations in improving livelihoods through capacity development, access to technology, financial and development services and linkage to markets.
